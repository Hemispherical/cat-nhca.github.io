Home

Northern hemisphere crime agency is a private institution which was created 18th january 2020 to curb the insecurity problems in the Northern region of the country. The areas affected mainly by insecurity are:

  • North Eastern Region
  • Coastal Region
  • Parts of Central Region
  • Parts of Eastern Region

About us

Northern hemisphere crime agency is located in various region in the country.The agency is a private agency which is sponsored and acknowledged by government. We advise people to inform us of any crime to provide effective solution to the problem.
